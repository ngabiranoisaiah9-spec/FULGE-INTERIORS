Design with a Ugandan Soul

Founded by Nakato Aisha and Mugisha Derrick, Fulge Interiors is Mbarara's premier interior design studio. With over a decade of experience transforming homes and businesses across Western Uganda, we fuse global trends with local craftsmanship.

Our approach is deeply collaborative — we listen, interpret, and translate your dreams into cohesive design narratives. From cozy residences in Katete to boutique hotels along Mbarara-Kabale road, every Fulge space echoes sophistication and warmth.

— Nakato Aisha, Creative Director & Lead Designer
